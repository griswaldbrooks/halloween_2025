================================================================================
                              SENSOR MAP
================================================================================

Motion Sensors (M):
  1. Spider Tunnel - Window scare (triggers video)
  2. Spider Tunnel - Ankle brusher (triggers pool noodle sweep)
  3. Trash Room - Robot arm #1 (triggers sweep)
  4. Trash Room - Robot arm #2 (triggers sweep)
  5. Spider's Lair - Entrance (triggers spider lunge + strobe)

Bluetooth Speaker Locations (S):
  1. Candy Room - Pleasant music
  2. Spider Tunnel - Scuttling/spider sounds
  3. Trash Room - Flies/dripping/groaning
  4. Victim Room - Moaning/crying (hidden in cocoons)
  5. Hatchery - Egg cracking/chittering
  6. Spider's Lair - Spider breathing/screech (LOUD)

Projectors (P):
  1. Spider Tunnel - Overhead (spider crawling video on walls)
  2. Spider's Lair - Floor/wall projection (baby spiders)
  3. Optional: Victim Room (dripping effect)

Fog Machine (F):
  1. Primary: Spider Tunnel entrance (creates atmosphere in webs)
  2. Optional: Spider's Lair (for final scare drama)
