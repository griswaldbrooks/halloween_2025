================================================================================
                              SENSOR MAP
================================================================================

Motion Sensors (M):
  1. Spider Tunnel - Window scare (triggers video)
  2. Spider Tunnel - Ankle brusher (triggers pool noodle sweep)
  3. Trash Room - Robot arm #1 (triggers sweep)
  4. Trash Room - Robot arm #2 (triggers sweep)
  5. Spider's Lair - Entrance (triggers spider lunge + strobe)

Bluetooth Speaker Locations (S):
  1. Candy Room - Pleasant music
  2. Spider Tunnel - Scuttling/spider sounds
  3. Trash Room - Flies/dripping/groaning
  4. Victim Room - Moaning/crying (hidden in cocoons)
  5. Hatchery - Egg cracking/chittering
  6. Spider's Lair - Spider breathing/screech (LOUD)

Projectors (P):
  1. Spider Tunnel - Overhead (spider crawling video on walls)
  2. Spider's Lair - Floor/wall projection (baby spiders)
  3. Optional: Victim Room (dripping effect)

Fog Machine (F):
  1. Primary: Spider Tunnel entrance (creates atmosphere in webs)
  2. Optional: Spider's Lair (for final scare drama)

================================================================================
                          SCAVENGER HUNT PATH
================================================================================

Chamber 1 (Candy Room):
  └─→ Clue/Token #1: On candy table (EASY - starts hunt)

Chamber 2 (Spider Tunnel):
  └─→ Item #2: Near window scare area (MEDIUM - must look around)

Chamber 3 (Trash Room):
  └─→ Item #3: Hidden in/on cocooned body (MEDIUM - must search)

Chamber 4 (Victim Room):
  └─→ Item #4: Behind hanging cocoon (MEDIUM - must navigate)

Chamber 5 (Hatchery):
  └─→ Item #5: Inside large egg sac container (HARD - unnerving reach)

Chamber 6 (Spider's Lair):
  └─→ Final Prize: After surviving spider attack (REWARD)

================================================================================
                          TRAFFIC FLOW NOTES
================================================================================

Estimated time per chamber:
  - Candy Room: 1-2 minutes (can linger)
  - Spider Tunnel: 3-5 minutes (twisting path)
  - Trash Room: 2-3 minutes
  - Victim Room: 2-3 minutes
  - Hatchery: 2-3 minutes
  - Spider's Lair: 1-2 minutes (quick scare and exit)

Total experience: 12-18 minutes

Group spacing: 3-5 minutes between groups
Capacity: 4-6 people per group

================================================================================
                          POWER REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

Candy Room:
  └─→ 1 outlet (lights, speaker)

Spider Tunnel:
  └─→ 3-4 outlets (fog machine, projector, LED strips, monitor,
                    ankle brusher motor, speaker)

Trash Room:
  └─→ 2-3 outlets (robot arms, lighting, speaker, scent setup)

Victim Room:
  └─→ 2-3 outlets (motors for twitching, lighting, speaker,
                    drip effect)

Hatchery:
  └─→ 2-3 outlets (LED controllers for eggs, fans, speaker,
                    UV lights)

Spider's Lair:
  └─→ 3-4 outlets (projector, animatronic spider, strobe,
                    speaker, lighting)

TOTAL: 13-18 outlets needed
       Plan for 6-8 power strips
       Use heavy-duty extension cords rated for load

================================================================================
                          EMERGENCY EXITS
================================================================================

Primary Emergency Exit: Spider's Lair (main exit)

Secondary Exits (Mark clearly):
  - Side exit in Spider Tunnel (if space allows)
  - Side exit in Trash Room or Victim Room (mid-point escape)

Emergency lighting: Battery-powered lights along exit paths

Staff access: Clear paths for monitors to enter each chamber
